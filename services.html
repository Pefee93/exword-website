Reddit Operations

Show up in the right threads, the right way.

We design and run Reddit activity that actually respects the communities you’re in. From posting strategy and comment routing to AMA support and long-term presence, we help your game become a trusted voice — not another promo post people scroll past.

Good for you if…

You know Reddit matters for your genre, but you don’t have the time (or the karma) to do it properly.

Example deliverables:

  • Subreddit mapping and opportunity report
  • Post and comment calendar
  • AMA planning and live support
  • Brand account guidelines and tone of voice
Community Management

Turn your Discord into a world players want to live in.

We help you build, launch, and maintain communities that feel like part of your game’s universe. From server structure and roles to daily engagement, events, and moderation support, we keep the vibes healthy and the conversation flowing.

Good for you if…

You have a Discord, but it feels more like a quiet lobby than a buzzing hub.

Example deliverables:

  • Server setup or restructuring
  • Community guidelines and role systems
  • Event concepts (playtests, Q&As, “ask the dev” sessions)
  • Ongoing engagement calendars and mod playbooks
YouTube Marketing

Put your game in front of players who actually watch.

We work with creators, formats, and channels that fit your game — not just whoever has the biggest numbers. From finding the right partners to shaping video concepts and measuring impact, we help you build sustainable visibility on YouTube.

Good for you if…

You’re tired of one-off sponsored videos that disappear the moment they’re uploaded.

Example deliverables:

  • Creator shortlist and outreach
  • Campaign concepts and briefs
  • Key messaging and talking points
  • Post-campaign performance recap
TikTok Campaigns

Short-form content that doesn’t feel cringe.

We help you show up natively on TikTok with concepts that match the platform’s rhythm. Whether it’s dev-log style content, meme formats, or creator-led clips, we build campaigns that feel like TikToks first and marketing second.

Good for you if…

You know your game belongs on TikTok but don’t want to become a full-time creator to make it happen.

Example deliverables:

  • Content and hook concepts
  • Posting schedule and format strategy
  • Creator sourcing for TikTok-native talent
  • Performance tracking and iteration recommendations
Influencer Networks

Build a circle of creators who actually care.

Instead of chasing one-off sponsorships, we help you build a network of creators who stay with your game over time. We map who makes sense for your genre, handle outreach and relationship management, and design ways to keep them excited to come back.

Good for you if…

You’d rather have 20 genuinely invested creators than one big channel that never mentions you again.

Example deliverables:

  • Creator landscape and tiered targeting
  • Outreach messaging and coordination
  • Relationship and key beat calendar
  • Long-term ambassador or partner program concepts
Content Production

Words, visuals, and clips that sound like you.

We create posts, scripts, short-form videos, and community assets that feel true to your game’s voice. Every piece of content is designed to live naturally on its platform, whether it’s a Reddit deep-dive, a Discord announcement, or a TikTok hook.

Good for you if…

You know what you want to say, but don’t have the time or bandwidth to package it properly.

Example deliverables:

  • Social copy and announcement packs
  • Short-form video scripts and shot ideas
  • Dev update and patch note copy
  • Visual templates for recurring formats
