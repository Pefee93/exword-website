ExWord started from a simple desire - to help game dev studios stand out in the vast sea of the video game market. Early on, we realized that organic and authentic marketing is the way forward, because of the unique relationship between the players and the games they enjoy. For them, games are not just products, but the world in which they leave a piece of themselves. Our goal was to turn that emotional connection into high-level marketing, and we successfully did so.

We started on Reddit, advising studios and developers on how to become valuable members of the platform and promote their games in ways that genuinely resonate with players. This brought us early success because we knew every corner of Reddit by heart. Being lifelong gamers and Redditors ourselves, we've spent countless hours participating in the communities and gaming-related discussions - making it second nature for us to navigate, understand, and connect.

As the studio grew, we expanded our expertise beyond Reddit - first into community management and Steam marketing, then into influencer campaigns, and most recently into visual content creation and TikTok marketing. With that, ExWord evolved into a full-stack marketing studio for games. However, we've never forgotten the roots that made us successful in the first place - authentic, high-quality content built on creativity, authenticity, and a genuine understanding of gaming culture. These principles are applied to every campaign we run.

Today, ExWord is an established gaming marketing studio with over 80 campaigns under our belt and rich experience across titles of all sizes - from indies to AAA. We're proud to be the go-to partner for game developers who want to build real, lasting connections with their players.
